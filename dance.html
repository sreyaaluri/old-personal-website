Sreya Aluri

Dance

Hobby · since 2007

I used to think of dance as something that’s just fun to do. However, this notion completely changed when I had my first live Kuchipudi (an Indian classical dance form) performance in which I explained the entire Ramayana (an ancient Indian epic) through just my dance.

Here is a picture taken right after my first live performance-

Over the past 10 years I became a certified Kuchipudi dancer, taught myself hip-hop and contemporary dance, and had almost 70 live performances. I’ve trained multiple people as a freelance instructor, choreographed several award-winning performances, and fallen completely in love with dance.

I’ve always danced to express myself — be it something to raise awareness about things important to me, something I’m doing just for fun, or something providing a means of catharsis.

Right from my first pair of gajjalu (ankle bells used while performing Indian classical dances) to my latest dance performance, I am grateful for every lesson I learnt, memory I made, and friend I met through dance.
